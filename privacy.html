Privacy Policy

Effective Date: July 18, 2026

1. Absolute Zero Telemetry

The Brink Labs develops offline-first applications, including MyneWallet. We operate on a strict "Zero Telemetry" architecture. We do not collect, harvest, transmit, or store any personal or financial data on external servers.

2. Local Storage Only

All data entered into our applications remains entirely on your physical device. We utilize local SQLite databases. If you uninstall the application or lose your device without executing a manual backup, your data cannot be recovered by us.

3. Google Drive Backups (Optional)

Users may opt-in to use the Google Drive API to create encrypted manual backups of their local database. This connection is exclusively established between the user's device and the user's personal Google Drive account. The Brink Labs has zero access to this data, the cryptographic keys, or your Google account credentials.

4. Contact Us

If you have any questions regarding this privacy policy, you may contact us directly at support@thebrinklabs.com.

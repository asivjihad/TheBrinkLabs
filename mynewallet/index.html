MyneWallet: Expense Tracker

Your money.

The exact purpose of the MyneWallet: Expense Tracker app is to provide a high-performance, strictly offline-first financial engine. It allows users to securely track daily expenses, manage budgets, and visualize spending.

Why we request user data: This app requests OAuth access to your Google Drive API strictly and exclusively to allow you to securely back up and restore your encrypted financial ledger. We do not access, read, or transmit your data to any third-party servers.
